FAQ

Questions Owners Ask Before They Start

How long does a value optimization engagement take?

Most engagements run 12-36 months. The Comprehensive Program runs 24-36 months; the Targeted Initiative Program runs 12-18 months; Executive Advisory is ongoing.

How is this different from management consulting?

Every initiative is selected and sequenced to move the valuation multiple a buyer or lender will actually pay for, not generic operational improvement. The framework is buyer-facing from day one.

Do you guarantee a higher multiple?

No credible advisor can. What we can commit to is disciplined execution on the drivers that have historically compounded multiple expansion: earnings quality, reduced concentration, documented operations, and a defensible growth narrative.

Can you work alongside our existing CPA and attorney?

Yes. RTO is fee-based and conflict-free, and the engagement is designed to coordinate with your existing advisors rather than replace them.

What size companies is this for?

Middle-market, privately-held companies, typically 5M-100M in revenue, where multi-year preparation can meaningfully expand the buyer pool and the multiple.
