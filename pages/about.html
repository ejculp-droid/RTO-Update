About RTO Advisory

Independent, fee-based advisory for owners navigating complex transition decisions.

Our mission

Help owners maximize return to owner through disciplined preparation, objective decision support, and coordinated execution during transition events.

Our approach

We focus on strategic readiness before transaction urgency. That means clearer priorities, stronger data quality, and reduced execution risk.

Who we serve

Founder-led and middle-market businesses preparing for succession, evaluating inbound interest, or planning a sale in the next 1-5 years.

How we work

Structured engagement plans, clear milestones, practical communication cadence, and direct coordination with your legal, tax, and financial advisors.
